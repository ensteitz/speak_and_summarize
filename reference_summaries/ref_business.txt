Sarah highlights the importance of purpose in action. After narrating a seemingly normal un unnoteworthy encounter with a flight attendant, she highlights what made that encounter stand out. The purpose of this story was to highlight how purpose is found at the intersection at who you are at your very best and the role in the world you are meant to play. The speaker digs into the importance of purpose for authenticity for business, citing various brands who have high ethos and thus are authentic to their purpose. Though she heavily emphasizes the dire need for purpose in an organization, she ends the talk with comforting words about the journey to finding the levels of purpose for different levels in the organization to increase strategic business decisions and overall productivity.
